## Your first board

When you open taskpapr for the first time, you'll see a clean, empty board with a default column layout:

- **To Do** — tasks waiting to be started
- **In Progress** — what you're working on right now
- **Done** — completed tasks

---

## Adding a task

1. Click the **+** button at the bottom of any column, or press `N` with that column focused.
2. Type your task name.
3. Press `Enter` to save, or `Escape` to cancel.

---

## Moving tasks

- **Drag and drop** — grab any task card and drag it to a different column or position.
- **Keyboard** — select a card and use `Ctrl+→` / `Ctrl+←` to move it between columns.
