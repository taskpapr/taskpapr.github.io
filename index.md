# taskpapr

**A minimal, paper-inspired task board. No noise, no friction.**

taskpapr is a distraction-free task management tool built around the way you actually think. No cluttered dashboards, no endless configuration. Just a clean board that feels like a sheet of paper on your desk.

---

## Why taskpapr?

Most task tools get in your way. They demand you learn a system before you can get anything done.

taskpapr starts simple and stays simple:

- **Drag-and-drop columns** — organise tasks the way that makes sense to you
- **Keyboard-first** — add and move tasks without reaching for the mouse
- **Paper aesthetic** — warm, calm, easy on the eyes
- **Fast** — no bloat, no telemetry, no dark patterns
